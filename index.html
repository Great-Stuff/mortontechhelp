Internet not working

  1. Click on the circle in bottom left corner. A menu with lots of apps should pop up
  2. Find Zscaler icon. If there is not a blue dot in the top right of the icon, wait until it appears
  3. With two fingers, click on the Zscaler icon. A smaller menu should pop up
  4. Click on App Info, the settings app should open
  5. Click on More Settings and Permissions, another window should open
  6. Click on Force Stop then Yes
  7. Open Zscaler again and refresh webpage until it loads
